# Privacy Policy Template

**Each vendor's app needs its own privacy policy URL.** Apple and Google both require this before accepting any submission.

This template is filled in per-vendor and hosted on the **vendor's website** (or a free static-page service like Notion / Carrd). Vendor's privacy policy URL goes in:
- App Store Connect → App Privacy section
- Google Play Console → Store listing → Privacy policy URL
- Inside the App's About / Settings page

**Vendor's lawyer should review** before publishing. This template is a strong starting point but is not legally binding without review.

---

# Privacy Policy for [VENDOR APP NAME]

Last updated: [DATE]

[VENDOR LEGAL NAME] ("we", "our", "us"), operating from [VENDOR ADDRESS] under the trade name [VENDOR DISPLAY NAME], operates the [VENDOR APP NAME] mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information we collect

When you use [VENDOR APP NAME], we collect the following:

### 1.1 Information you provide directly
- **Contact information** — name, email address, phone number — when you place an order or chat with us
- **Delivery information** — street address, building/unit number, delivery notes
- **Payment information** — handled by our payment processors (Stripe, Midtrans, PayPal, etc.), we never see or store your card details
- **Order content** — items in your cart, special requests, scheduled delivery times
- **Chat messages** — your messages to our shop, plus our replies

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device information** — phone model, OS version, app version (for support and crash reports)
- **Location** (with your permission) — your approximate location to estimate delivery cost and time
- **Usage data** — which pages you visit, which items you view, which buttons you tap (for improving the app)

### 1.3 Information we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your social security number, government ID, or financial account numbers
- We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or other apps without your explicit permission
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:
- Process and deliver your orders
- Communicate with you about orders, promotions, and updates
- Operate the loyalty program (if you participate)
- Improve the app and our service
- Comply with legal obligations (tax records, food safety reporting)

We do NOT sell your information to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.

## 3. Third-party services we use

To run [VENDOR APP NAME], we share specific data with the following service providers:

- **StreetLocal** — the platform powering this app. Hosts our menu, processes orders, and stores customer data on secure servers. See https://streetlocal.live/privacy for their privacy practices.
- **Supabase** — database and authentication, located in [REGION]. SOC 2 Type 2 certified.
- **Payment processors** — [LIST WHICH ONES YOU USE: Stripe, Midtrans, PayPal, etc.]. Each handles its own privacy policy; we share only the transaction details required to process your payment.
- **Apple / Google** (for app delivery) — your device identifier and app crash data, per their standard developer terms.
- **Resend** — email delivery service for order confirmations.

We do not share your information with anyone else. We do not allow advertising networks to track you.

## 4. Data retention

- **Order history** — kept for 7 years (tax/accounting requirements)
- **Chat history** — kept for 2 years, then auto-deleted
- **Loyalty stamps** — kept while you are an active customer
- **Account data** — deleted within 30 days of account closure request

## 5. Your rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Correct** — fix any inaccuracies
- **Delete** — request account closure and data deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Withdraw consent** — stop receiving marketing messages at any time
- **Object** — to specific uses of your data (e.g. profiling, automated decisions)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any right, email [VENDOR SUPPORT EMAIL] or message us in the app's chat.

We will respond within 30 days of receiving a request.

## 6. Children's privacy

[VENDOR APP NAME] is not intended for users under 13 years old (or 16 in EU jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child, we delete it immediately.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures:
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS 1.3)
- All data encrypted at rest in our database
- Database access restricted by row-level security policies
- Two-factor authentication for staff accounts
- Regular security audits

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours and report to the relevant authorities as required by law.

## 8. International data transfers

If you are located in the EU/EEA: by using the app, you consent to your data being processed in [REGION], which may be outside the EEA. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers and Supabase's GDPR compliance.
